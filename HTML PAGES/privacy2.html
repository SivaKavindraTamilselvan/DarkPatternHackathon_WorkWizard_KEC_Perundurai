PRIVACY POLICY

  • Amazon collects various types of personal information from users to operate, provide, develop, and improve its products and services.
  • The information falls into three categories:
        1.Information You Give:Users provide information related to Amazon services, such as personal details, during searches, shopping, account setup, and other interactions.
        2.Automatic Information:Amazon automatically collects and stores data on users' interactions with its services. This includes information about content use, device metrics, IP addresses, login details, and more. Cookies and identifiers are used for this purpose.
        3.Information from Other Sources:Amazon may receive additional information from third parties, such as updated delivery details, account information from affiliates, and credit history information from credit bureaus.
  • Amazon utilizes personal information for various purposes, including purchase and delivery of products, improving services, providing personalized recommendations, complying with legal obligations, communicating with users, advertising, fraud prevention, and managing credit risks.
  • Amazon emphasizes security measures to protect users' personal information during transmission and maintains safeguards for data storage. Users can access and manage their information through the "Your Account" section.
  • Amazon does not sell customers' personal information but may share it with third parties for specific purposes, such as transactions, service providers, and business transfers.
  • Users have choices regarding the information they provide, preferences for communication, and options to manage cookies and identifiers.
  • Amazon's Privacy Notice is subject to change, and users are encouraged to check for updates regularly.
  • The privacy practices are in accordance with applicable laws, and Amazon commits to protecting customer information.
